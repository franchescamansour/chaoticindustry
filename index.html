RILEY STEWART

Riley Stewart studied English Literature and Theatre at the University of Ottawa and has worked as a playwright, actor and musician since 1987. His credits as a musician include the Great Canadian Theatre Company production of Wendy Lill’s Sisters, Triangle Vitale’s productions of Signale D’Alarme and Les Consciences Fragile, the National Arts Centre productions of Snowlands and The Whore of Barcelona, as well as the full score and lyrics for many Odyssey Theatre productions, including Don Juan and The Green Bird. Riley was also composer and creator of the full-length musical Exhibit A, which was produced in association with the National Arts Centre and the Great Canadian Theatre Company. His music has appeared in productions in Montreal, Toronto and Vancouver, and in numerous films and television series, including The Secret Garden and Sweet Tarts Takeaway. He has also provided music for a number of computer games, including Mode and Midnight Stranger. Riley currently lives in Ottawa, Canada.
